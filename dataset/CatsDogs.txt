Task is to classify an audio recording as either cat or dog.

The time series represent the change in amplitude over time in the pressence of either cat or dog sounds.
The data set has been truncated to the shortest instance.

Original data can be found here:
https://www.kaggle.com/mmoreaux/audio-cats-and-dogs

Original paper:
Takahashi N, Gygli M, Pfister B, Van Gool L. Deep convolutional neural networks and data augmentation for acoustic event detection. arXiv preprint arXiv:1604.07160. 2016 Apr 25.
